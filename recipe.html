Bufflo Crack Chicken Dip Recipe

This is one of my favorite recipes because, I have alway's loved spicy food, especially bufflo. I came across this recipe one day when i was scrolling through facebook (thats what my entire facebook feed is) and as soon as I saw it I knew I knew i wanted to make it. This recipe goes best with Truco On The Border Tortilla Chips that you can buy at walmart. It also goes great with celery.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded rotisserie chicken or about 1lb chicken breasts cooked
  • 1 8oz package cream cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup mayo and sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb bacon cooked and chopped

How To Make

  1. Lightly spray with cooking spray a 2qt baking dish, set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Combine all the ingredients starting with the cooked chicken up to the minced garlic. Stir well to combine. Transfer to the prepared baking dish.
  4. Top with the cooked chopped bacon.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, until hot and bubbly.
  6. Serve immediately.

(Optional) Make Your Own Bufflo Sauce

  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • pinch granulated garlic (optional)
